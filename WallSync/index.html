HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
Then it just works.

No complicated setup. WallSynq runs silently in your system tray while you focus on what matters.

STEP 01
🔗
Connect your platform

Sign in with Spotify or YouTube Music in one click. WallSynq only gets read-only access to what's currently playing. Your data stays yours.

STEP 02
▶️
Play music like always

Open your favourite playlist and hit play. WallSynq runs quietly in the background — you'll forget it's even there.

STEP 03
🖼
Watch your desktop transform

Every new song pulls its album art and sets it as your wallpaper — with a smooth cross-fade. Your desktop tells the story of what you're listening to.

FEATURES

Built for people who
actually listen.

🎨
Album art, full screen

Every album has a visual world behind it. puts that artwork exactly where you'll see it — your desktop background.

🔄
Real-time auto switching

Song changes? Desktop changes. Automatically, instantly. WallSynq polls your music so you never have to touch a setting.

✨
Smooth cross-fade

No jarring flickers. Every wallpaper change uses a cinematic cross-fade so your desktop always feels intentional.

🎵
Your existing playlists

No need to rebuild your library. Keep using Spotify or YouTube Music exactly as you do today — WallSynq just listens.

🖥
Multi-monitor support

Running two or three screens? Sync the same artwork across all of them, or configure each display individually.

⚙️
Lives in your system tray

WallSynq doesn't clutter your taskbar. It sits in the system tray using minimal CPU — a ghost on your system.
